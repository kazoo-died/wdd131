Recipe Haven – Site Plan

Author: Daniel Rivard

Site Name

Recipe Haven

I chose the name “Recipe Haven” because the website is designed to be a comforting and creative space where users can discover, share, and save their favorite recipes. The name suggests warmth, community, and a love for cooking.

Optional domain: recipehaven.net

Site Purpose

The purpose of Recipe Haven is to create a simple and visually appealing website where visitors can browse delicious recipes, submit their own, and save their favorites for later. The site aims to bring together home cooks who enjoy discovering new meals and sharing their own culinary creations.

Scenarios

  • “What are some quick and easy dinner recipes I can make with simple ingredients?”
  • “How can I submit and save my own recipes to view later?”

Color Scheme

Primary and accent colors selected for the website:

  • Burnt Orange (#d35400): Used for headings, buttons, and navigation highlights.
  • Soft Cream (#fffaf5): Used for background color to give a warm, inviting feel.
  • Charcoal Gray (#333): Used for text to ensure good readability and contrast.

Typography

  • Poppins – for headings (modern and clean look)
  • Open Sans – for body text (simple and highly readable)

These fonts complement each other and create a friendly, contemporary design suitable for a recipe-focused website.
